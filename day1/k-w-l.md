# K W L chart

KWL is a graphic organizer with three columns that is used during the beginning of a lesson..

Typically stand's for **Know** - **what I WANT to know** - **Learnt** chart

Link to a KWL chart [here](k-w-l.pdf)

### Example

| Know | Wonder  | Learn  |
|------|---------|--------|
| sql  | bash    |        |
| tcl/tk | python|        |
